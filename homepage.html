Welcome to Canvas to Google Tasks Sync

This guide will help you set up the extension and sync your Canvas assignments to Google Tasks.

Step 1: Retrieve Your Canvas API Token

  1. Log in to your Canvas account.
  2. Go to Account > Settings.
  3. Scroll down to the Approved Integrations section.
  4. Click + New Access Token.
  5. Provide a purpose (e.g., "Google Tasks Sync") and click Generate Token.
  6. Copy the token and save it securely.

Step 2: Configure the Extension

  1. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar.
  2. Go to the Options page.
  3. Paste your Canvas API token into the Canvas Token field.
  4. Click Save.

Step 3: Sync Your Assignments

  1. Click the Sync button in the extension popup.
  2. Your Canvas assignments will be synced to Google Tasks.

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues, please refer to the Options page for troubleshooting or contact support.
